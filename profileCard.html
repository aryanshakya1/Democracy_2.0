Narendra Modi

Biography: Narendra Modi, born on September 17, 1950, in Vadnagar, India, is a prominent Indian politician who has been serving as the Prime Minister of India since May 2014. Before his premiership, he was the Chief Minister of Gujarat from 2001 to 2014 and is currently the Member of Parliament for Varanasi.

As Chief Minister of Gujarat, Modi's administration was noted for its policies towards economic development, but his tenure was also marked by controversy, particularly the 2002 Gujarat riots. His leadership style and governance model have been both praised and criticized.

Modi's tenure as Prime Minister has seen significant policy decisions, including the implementation of the Goods and Services Tax (GST), demonetization of high-value currency notes, and efforts to improve infrastructure and digitalization in India
