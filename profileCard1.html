Draupadi Murmu

Biography:Draupadi Murmu is an Indian politician who has been serving as the 15th President of India since July 25, 2022. She is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and is notable for being the first person from the tribal community to hold the office of President in India. Additionally, she is the second woman after Pratibha Patil to become President and the first President born after India's independence. Prior to her presidency, she served as the Governor of Jharkhand from 2015 to 2021.
